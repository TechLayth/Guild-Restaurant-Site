About Papa Comfort

Welcome to the world of Papa Comfort. Here it's all about good food and good vibes. We set the bar high with quality ingredients and masterful cooking. With Papa is only perfection. Papa Comfort started with his burgers in the backyard of his house with his family. They could not believe how good it was. he started selling to friends and shared it with co-workers. The feedback was great and he went on to start selling in his first brick and motor. The town couldn't get enough and Papa Comfort Foods was a hit. From his world class burgers to now a menu of 6 high quality items you can be sure that any ambitious foodie will be satiated. When someone eats our food they make the face, they lean back, they relax. Oh yeah baby like Papa says he is in the ComfortZone and we want to help you get there. Here you can relax after a long day at work, a family dinner or for one of those days you are just feeling down. Don't worry, come on over we have a recipe for smiles.
